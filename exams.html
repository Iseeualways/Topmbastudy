Top MBA Entrance Exams in India

Choosing the right MBA entrance exam is a crucial step for students aspiring to pursue management education at India’s top business schools. These exams serve as the gateway to prestigious MBA and PGDM programs offered by the IIMs, IITs, private universities, and leading autonomous B-schools across the country. Each exam evaluates a candidate’s aptitude in areas such as quantitative ability, logical reasoning, verbal skills, and data interpretation — key competencies for a successful career in management.

Below is a comprehensive overview of the most popular and competitive MBA entrance exams in India, along with the institutions that accept them and their unique features.

MBA Entrance Exams in India

Exam Name Full Form Accepted By Highlights
CAT Common Admission Test IIMs, IITs, and top-tier private B-schools Conducted by the IIMs, CAT is India’s most competitive MBA exam, testing candidates on quantitative, verbal, and analytical skills.
XAT Xavier Aptitude Test XLRI and 150+ institutes Conducted by XLRI Jamshedpur, XAT emphasizes decision-making and logical reasoning along with standard aptitude sections.
NMAT NMIMS Management Aptitude Test NMIMS and 50+ B-schools Conducted by GMAC, NMAT offers flexibility with multiple attempts and focuses on language, reasoning, and quantitative skills.
MAT Management Aptitude Test 600+ business schools Conducted by AIMA, MAT is held multiple times a year and is widely accepted by top private universities.
CMAT Common Management Admission Test AICTE-approved institutions A national-level test evaluating candidates across quantitative, logical, and general awareness sections.
SNAP Symbiosis National Aptitude Test Symbiosis International (Deemed University) Conducted by SIU, SNAP is required for admission to all Symbiosis management institutes in India.
ATMA AIMS Test for Management Admissions 500+ B-schools in India Recognized by AICTE, ATMA focuses on analytical reasoning, verbal ability, and quantitative aptitude.
TISSNET Tata Institute of Social Sciences National Entrance Test TISS Mumbai and campuses For admission to TISS MBA-equivalent programs in HRM, ODCL, and social entrepreneurship.
KMAT Karnataka Management Aptitude Test B-schools in Karnataka State-level MBA entrance exam accepted by top management colleges in Karnataka.
PGCET (MBA) Post Graduate Common Entrance Test State universities & affiliated colleges Conducted by state authorities (like KEA in Karnataka), offering MBA seats in government and private colleges.
